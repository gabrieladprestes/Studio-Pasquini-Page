Who we are and What we created

We are a global graphic design studio specializing in creating impactful communication solutions for packaging, digital mockups, and digital marketing. With a presence in Italy, the United States, and Brazil, we deliver excellence across borders.

We intentionally maintain a small team with immense creative potential, dedicated to delivering projects with focused and personalized service to our customers and partners. Here, you speak directly with the designer, and the owners are actively involved in the business.

Our work model results in agility, a focused approach to your project, and a personal touch in our service that makes a significant difference.

With 21 years of experience creating design projects for both small and large companies, we can start from scratch, building a new brand for a new business, or engage in the innovation process by developing packaging for some of the largest food companies in the world.

To be the best at whatever we set out to do, with absolute focus on our activities, ensuring the best services for our clients and the opportunity for a better future for all our employees.

Our values

Ownership mindset

Committed to results, deeply knowledgeable about what they do, and has a holistic view. Acts with determination, discipline, and attention to detail. Hands-on, always striving to be the best at what they do and never gives up.

Always available and leads by example. Speaks up and refuses to accept or remain silent about things that don’t work well or could be improved. Pays close attention to expenses and the saving of every cent. Fully engaged with the organization's culture.

Simplicity

Gets things done in a simple and practical way, is hands-on, goes straight to the point, simplifies and cuts through bureaucracy while respecting the rules.

Humility

Knows how to listen, is attentive, values others’ opinions, and believes it doesn’t matter who did it—what matters is that we did it together. Isn’t afraid to ask questions or admit not knowing something. Is neither arrogant nor vain and always acts with respect. Doesn’t care about status and doesn’t think they have all the answers. Prioritizes 'we' over 'I'.

Determination

Is driven, delivers outstanding results, and honors commitments. Makes things happen, seeks solutions to problems, and engages people toward a common goal. Has a sense of urgency, an ownership mindset, and never gives up.

Availability

Is receptive, approachable, available, with no set day or hour, always ready, and prioritizes work. Is open to new things, to change, and motivated for new challenges.
